This platform could generate videos for education, marketing, entertainment, and news, all tailored to the Arabic-speaking audience. The UAE, with its strong media industry and focus on digital transformation, is an ideal market. A solo developer could start by fine-tuning an existing open-source video generation model (like the architecture behind HappyHorse) on a dataset of Arabic video clips and text prompts. The platform would need to be mindful of cultural sensitivities and ensure content aligns with Islamic values and local regulations.

A chatbot trained on Islamic finance principles, regulations, and product details could automate responses to common queries, provide personalized financial advice, and even process basic transactions.
